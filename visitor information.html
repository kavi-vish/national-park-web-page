Parking Hours


Enchanted Falls National Park offers varying park hours throughout the year to provide visitors with the opportunity to experience the park's beauty in different seasons. Please note that these hours are subject to change, and it's advisable to check the official park website or contact the Visitor Center for the most up-to-date information.

Spring (March - May):
Weekdays: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Weekends: 8:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Summer (June - August):
Daily: 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Fall (September - November):
Weekdays: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Weekends: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Winter (December - February):
Weekdays: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Weekends: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Please be aware that the park may close during severe weather conditions or for special events. Additionally, entrance fees may vary depending on the time of year and specific activities. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation in preserving the natural beauty of Enchanted Falls National Park. Enjoy your visit!

Entrance Fee





To support the conservation and maintenance efforts of Enchanted Falls National Park, an entrance fee is required for all visitors. Your contribution ensures the preservation of this natural haven, allowing future generations to enjoy its beauty. Please find the current entrance fees below:

Individuals (ages 16 and older):
Day Pass: $10.00
Annual Pass: $40.00

Children (ages 15 and under):
Free (must be accompanied by a paying adult)

Senior Citizens (ages 62 and older):
Day Pass: $5.00
Annual Pass: $20.00

Active Military (with valid ID):
Free

Group Rates (10 or more people):
Per Person: $8.00

Please note that fees are subject to change, and discounts may be available for educational groups, seniors, and other special circumstances. All fees collected contribute directly to the preservation, maintenance, and enhancement of Enchanted Falls National Park. Thank you for your support, and we hope you have a wonderful and enriching experience during your visit.

Safety Tips


Ensuring the safety of all visitors is paramount at Enchanted Falls National Park. Please adhere to the following safety guidelines to enhance your visit and promote a secure environment. Stay on designated trails to prevent getting lost and protect delicate ecosystems. Inform a reliable person of your outdoor plans, including expected return times. Carry essential gear such as water, appropriate clothing, and navigation tools. Check and stay aware of current and forecasted weather conditions, avoiding visits during severe weather. Respect wildlife by keeping a safe distance and knowing how to react in case of an encounter. Be cautious near water bodies, follow safety guidelines, and carry a first aid kit. Respect campfire rules and fully extinguish fires. Familiarize yourself with park rules and regulations, and follow guidelines for camping, photography, and other activities. Save emergency contact numbers and be prepared to provide accurate location information in case of an emergency. Stay informed about park conditions, closures, and alerts. Whenever possible, explore the park with a companion for added safety. Follow safety recommendations from park staff and participate in orientation programs or guided tours for additional information. By following these guidelines, you contribute to a positive and secure experience for yourself and fellow visitors, ensuring that everyone can enjoy the beauty of Enchanted Falls National Park safely.
